  <div class="srs-section">

    <p><strong>AgriConnect: Digital Platform for Value-Added Agricultural Products</strong></p>
  </div>

  <div class="srs-section">
    <h3>2. Problem Statement</h3>
    <p>
      To support farmers in producing and marketing value-added agricultural products by creating
      a web-based platform that connects them with global buyers, thereby promoting rural entrepreneurship.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="srs-section">
    <h3>3. Objectives</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Empower farmers to sell processed and handmade agricultural products.</li>
      <li>Provide a digital marketplace for global buyers and rural producers.</li>
      <li>Ensure transparency and communication between users.</li>
      <li>Promote sustainable rural entrepreneurship.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="srs-section">
    <h3>4. Scope</h3>
    <p>The system supports three main users: Admin, Farmer, and Buyer.</p>
    <ul>
      <li><b>Admin:</b> Manages users, transactions, and platform maintenance.</li>
      <li><b>Farmer:</b> Adds and manages product listings.</li>
      <li><b>Buyer:</b> Browses and purchases products, provides feedback.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="srs-section">
    <h3>5. Functional Requirements</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Secure login and registration for all users.</li>
      <li>Admin can monitor and manage accounts and transactions.</li>
      <li>Farmers can add, update, and delete products.</li>
      <li>Buyers can browse, order, and review products.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="srs-section">
    <h3>6. Non-Functional Requirements</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><b>Performance:</b> Support 500+ concurrent users.</li>
      <li><b>Security:</b> Encrypt user data and transactions.</li>
      <li><b>Usability:</b> Responsive and intuitive UI.</li>
      <li><b>Reliability:</b> Ensure 99% uptime.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
